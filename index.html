Why I Love Drawing

Drawing is more than a pastime for me. It's a way to express ideas, tell stories, and relax my mind. I started sketching when I was a child, and over time, it became my favorite creative outlet.

Tools I Use

Pencil & Paper

The simplest tools are still my favorites. A basic graphite pencil and a sketchbook go a long way in capturing inspiration.

Digital Tablet

For digital art, I use a drawing tablet. It allows for more flexibility with colors, brushes, and editing.

Benefits of Drawing

  • Boosts creativity
  • Improves focus and patience
  • Reduces stress
  • Helps visualize thoughts and ideas
